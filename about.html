WHO WE ARE

Xpand Ventures is a next-generation media innovation and content company powered by creativity, data, and AI . We design smart, scalable media experiences that spark connection, accelerate growth, and deliver results you can actually measure.

ROHIT MACARIUS

(The Founder)

After nearly 23 years spent at the pinnacle of India's most prominent media and entertainment houses, Rohit Macarius saw not a digital challenge, but a transformational opportunity to redefine client growth. He recognized that the shift to digital platforms offered the unprecedented chance to merge the unbeatable reach and brand authority of traditional media (which he mastered at companies like The Times of India, Star Entertainment, and Radio Mirchi) with the precision, efficiency, and real-time measurability of a digital space. Rohit founded Xpand Ventures, intending to achieve success and growth; he envisioned an agency that could eliminate guesswork, ensuring every client's investment was a high-return asset. His goal is to leverage his expertise in large-scale strategy and successful execution to build the next generation of marketing where growth is not accidental, but strategically guaranteed and continuously optimized.
